kamal mouassa

Après le service nationale, et sa formation de professeur d'EPS au CREPS d'Annaba, a commencé sa carrière en tant qu'entraîneur des catégories jeunes du 2e club de sa ville natale à savoir OMSG (OLYMPIQUE DE GUELMA), puis les juniors de l'ESG et après a pris les mains de l'équipe seniors de l'OMSG en 1979/1980 (division d'honneur)

En tant qu'entraîneur, il remporte la Coupe de la CAF 2001 avec la JS Kabylie. Il réalise également trois accessions, avec l'ES Guelma (1981-1982), l'ASM Oran (2013-2014) et l'USM Blida (2014-2015).



Kamal Moussa’s First Training Session with ES Guelma: A New Chapter Begins.

Kamal Moussa marked his debut training session with ES Guelma, bringing a wave of excitement and optimism to the club. The session was filled with energy and determination, setting the tone for what promises to be a transformative period under Moussa’s guidance.

Under the watchful eye of Kamal Moussa, ES Guelma’s players were put through an intense and focused training session that emphasized discipline, tactical awareness, and teamwork. Moussa’s hands-on approach and motivational style quickly resonated with the squad, as he challenged them to push their limits and embrace a winning mentality.



The Heartbeat of ES Guelma: Passionate Supporters Bring the Fun

The vibrant supporters of ES Guelma are the lifeblood of the club, turning every matchday into a festival of football and camaraderie. Known for their unwavering loyalty and infectious energy, fans fill the stands with chants, banners, and a sea of club colors, creating an atmosphere that fuels the players’ performance on the pitch.

Off the field, the fun never stops, as supporters gather in local cafes and streets to share stories, celebrate victories, and dream of future glories. Their dedication goes beyond cheering—it’s a shared love for the team that unites the community, making ES Guelma not just a club, but a cherished symbol of pride and joy.
